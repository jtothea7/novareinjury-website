● Medical Director

Dr. Javier Sosa, MD Medical Director

Dr. Sosa is a board-certified Family Medicine physician with over 38 years of experience. He graduated from Universidad Central del Caribe School of Medicine in 1987 and completed his residency at St. Mary's Hospital.

As Medical Director at Novaré™, Dr. Sosa provides the physician oversight that makes the practice a licensed Medical Corporation rather than a standalone chiropractic office. This matters because certain insurance determinations, prescription coordination, and complex case management require medical oversight that chiropractic licensure alone doesn't cover.

Dr. Sosa integrates traditional medicine with functional and regenerative approaches. He is fluent in English and Spanish and has served the Fort Myers community for decades.

Every patient gets digital x-rays on the first visit. When disc involvement is suspected, we require MRI before starting spinal decompression. Dr. Bracic reads every scan himself. No guessing.

Equipment That Works

$25,000 flexion-distraction spinal tables. DRX9000 and AccuSPINA decompression systems. AI-guided Class IV lasers (first clinic in Florida selected for next-gen AI laser research). Knee-On-Trac decompression. Shockwave therapy. This isn't a clinic with one adjustment table and a billing department.

Medical Oversight Built In

Documentation That Holds Up
