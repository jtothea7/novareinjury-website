Should You See a Doctor After a Car Accident?

Yes. Even if you feel fine at the scene. Adrenaline can mask whiplash, disc injuries, and nerve damage for hours or days. In Florida, you have 14 days from the date of your accident to see a qualifying medical provider or risk losing your PIP benefits entirely. Not reduced. Denied.

Reasons to see a car accident doctor at Novaré™:

  • Neck pain, stiffness, or headaches after a collision
  • Back pain or radiating leg pain that started after impact
  • Numbness, tingling, or weakness in arms or legs
  • Dizziness, jaw pain, or difficulty concentrating
  • You need PIP documentation within 14 days
  • You need legal-grade records for your attorney
  • You were in a collision and feel fine (get checked anyway)
