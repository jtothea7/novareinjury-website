Stimulates Nerve Regeneration

Class IV laser light penetrates to the nerve level, stimulating mitochondrial activity and ATP production in damaged nerve cells. This promotes repair and regeneration.

Increases Blood Flow

Laser therapy promotes angiogenesis, forming new blood vessels to bring oxygen and nutrients to nerves that have been starved of circulation.

Reduces Pain Without Drugs

Unlike medications that mask symptoms, laser therapy addresses the nerve damage itself. No pills, no injections, no side effects from long-term medication use.

Combined with Manual Therapy

Manual therapy and nerve gliding exercises complement laser treatment to improve nerve mobility and reduce compression.
